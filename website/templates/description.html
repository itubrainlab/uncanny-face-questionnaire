Explanation of the questions

You will be rating each image on a scale between two descriptions.

The scales are written here with definitions:

Fictional - Real

Fictional: invented for the purposes of fiction.
Real: not imitation or artificial - genuine.

Human-made - Human-like

Human-made: made by a human.
Human-like: looking and behaving like a human.

Eerie - Ordinary

Eerie: strange and frightening.
Ordinary: with no special or distinctive features - normal.

Unsettling - Plain

Unsettling: causing anxiety or uneasiness - disturbing.
Plain: not beautiful or attractive, simple or basic.

Creepy - Dull

Creepy: causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease.
Dull: lacking interest or excitement.

Hair-raising - Unemotional

Hair-raising: alarming, astonishing, or frightening, makes your hair stand up.
Unemotional: not giving strong feelings.

Hostile - Friendly

Hostile: showing or feeling opposition or dislike.
Friendly: kind and pleasant.

Grumpy - Cheerful

Grumpy: bad-tempered and irritable.
Cheerful: noticeably happy and optimistic.

Cold-hearted - Warm-hearted

Cold-hearted: lacking affection or warmth - unfeeling.
Warm-hearted: sympathetic and kind.

If the character doesn't fit either of the descriptions, pick the one that is the closest. The scales are the same for all images.
